Project Overview

LÜMA is a luxury organic skincare brand that needed a complete visual identity and social media strategy. I developed their brand aesthetic, created all visual content, and established their social media presence with a minimalist, nude-and-gold theme that reflects their premium positioning in the market.

Client

LÜMA Organic Skincare

Timeline

1 month (2025)

Tools Used

Photoshop, Ai Images Generator, Chat GPT, Canva, Instagram, Google Analytics

Instagram Stories Strategy

For LÜMA’s Instagram Stories, I designed a visually cohesive and engaging story experience that aligned with the brand's soft nude and gold aesthetic. Stories featured product highlights, digital price lists, brand values, why us segments, interactive polls, tutorials, and behind-the-scenes clips. Each story was crafted to boost engagement, reinforce brand values, and drive traffic to the shop. Highlights were organized using branded covers to ensure consistency and visual appeal across all story categories.



AI Content Creation

For LÜMA's visual content, I integrated AI tools ethically to streamline the creative process and enhance productivity without compromising originality. Using AI platforms, I generated visual mockups, refined product compositions, and explored creative directions that aligned with the brand’s aesthetic. Every piece of content was carefully reviewed and customized to maintain LÜMA’s authenticity and ensure brand consistency.

Sales
