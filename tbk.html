Project Overview

I art directed and styled a bold and dramatic food photoshoot for The Butcher Knife, a steakhouse restaurant brand. My focus was on using intense lighting, deep shadows, and high contrast to capture the richness and texture of their signature dishes. The photos were tailored for social media platforms, and I also created custom graphics specifically for Instagram to enhance the brand’s visual identity in digital content.


Results & Impact

The photoshoot delivered a dramatic and visually captivating set of images tailored for digital marketing and social platforms. The high-end, moody visual style resonated well with the brand’s audience.
